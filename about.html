Our Story

Handcrafted jewelry, made to carry memory.

Since our founding in Bengaluru, Redes has combined traditional metalsmithing with contemporary design to craft pieces that are intended to be worn, cherished and passed down. We source responsibly and craft intentionally.

Our Mission

We believe jewelry is a language for memory. Our mission is to design pieces that tell stories, created ethically and skillfully so they can be part of your family narrative for generations.

  • ✓
    Ethical Sourcing
    We partner with trusted suppliers and favor recycled metals and ethically mined stones when possible.
  • ✦
    Artisanal Craft
    Traditional techniques paired with modern design thinking — each piece is shaped by hand.
  • ⚑
    Transparent Pricing
    Clear, honest cost breakdowns for bespoke projects so clients know exactly what they're paying for.

Craft Philosophy

We design with longevity in mind. That means picking the best materials, thinking through wear patterns, and offering full servicing so pieces can be worn for life.

Materials
Gold, platinum, responsibly sourced gemstones and durable findings.
Design Process
Sketch → CAD → Prototype → Final craft with client feedback loops.

Our Journey

A short timeline highlighting key moments in our history.

2009

Founding

Redes began as a weekend studio in Bengaluru focused on custom designs for local clients.
2013

First Atelier

We opened our first dedicated atelier and trained apprentices in hand-setting and finishing.
2018

Sustainability Pledge

Our commitment to recycled metals and supplier audits became a formal part of our company policy.
2023

Worldwide Clients

We expanded to private viewings and shipped custom pieces internationally.
