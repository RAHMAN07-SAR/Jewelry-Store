Frequently Asked

How do I book a bespoke appointment?
Use the form above, select 'Bespoke Design' and provide preferred dates. We'll reply with available slots.
Do you offer repairs?
Yes — we provide repair and servicing. Please include photos in your message for an initial assessment.
Can I bring my own stones?
We can set client-supplied stones. We'll evaluate suitability and provide a secure handling process.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship insured parcels worldwide. Customs and taxes are handled at checkout for international orders.
Do you provide valuations?
We provide valuations for insured pieces and upon request for bespoke projects.
